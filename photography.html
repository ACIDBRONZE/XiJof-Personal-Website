EQUIPMENT


XIAN DIVINAGRACIA

I use a Canon Powershot S5IS which is my father's camera that he bought back in 2007. I am fond for the "vintage and nostalgic" feel when I took pictures with it and it is now one of my styles that I used in photography. I use my camera usually for occassions or when I am in travelling.
Additionally I use my Samsung A5 5G phone whenever I didn't bring my camera or for everyday life.

JOFFER ESCOTO

I keep things simple with my Realme 8 5G camera, relying on its impressive 64MP main lens to capture sharp, vibrant images. While it may not be a high-end DSLR, the phone’s camera delivers great results, especially when paired with the power of my steady hand. I focus on natural light and clean compositions, using stability and focus to get the most out of every shot—making each photo feel as professional as possible, even with minimal gear.

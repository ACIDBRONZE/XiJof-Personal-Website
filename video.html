XIAN DIVINAGRACIA

Depending on the deadline or my mood, I use CapCut because it is fast and easy to use, plus it has many tutorials available online, making it easy to achieve results comparable to average editors. However, when I want to be serious about editing, I use a combination of Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve for big or lengthy projects.

For animation, aside from PowerPoint, I use After Effects for motion graphics and Blender for low-poly 3D animation. My goal is to create a simple animated short and a music visualizer, similar to those seen in video games and music festivals.

JOFFER ESCOTO

I do all my editing straight from my Realme 8 5G phone, using the CapCut app. It’s not the usual computer-based editing workflow, but it works perfectly for me. CapCut is easy to use, and the phone’s performance is solid enough to handle most of my editing needs, whether it's cutting videos, adding effects, or tweaking audio. It’s all about making the process simple and efficient, and with my phone, I can edit on the go or from the comfort of wherever I happen to be.
